Broth

We import our broth from our local partners in Chongqing, China, to ensure that the taste is as authentic as possible.

Meat

Local grass-fed beef. Free-range chickens. Fish captured using sustainable practices.

Vegetables

To reduce our strain on the environment, all vegetables, where possible, are grown in the UK.

Our Vision

Located in the South-West of China, Chongqing was previously part of the Sichuan province, a region known for its spicy food. The most famous of which is hot pot. We aim to deliver the same high-quality experience and authentic flavours in an approachable way.

Our Accolades

Featured on BBC One's Master Chef as a location for contestants to get experience in a production kitchen. Listed in the London Times' top 10 'Must See' restaurants in London.
